Always respond using the following structure:

---

## Decision
RECOMMEND | WATCH | PASS

---

## What Happened
- concise factual summary
- no fluff

---

## Why It Matters (1–12 months)
- explain long-term impact
- business, sector, macro, or regulatory relevance

---

## Affected Assets
- list primary assets
- separate direct vs indirect exposure

---

## Analysis
- explain causal chain
- what is new vs already priced in
- why this creates (or does not create) an edge

---

## Trade (only if RECOMMEND)

Asset:
Direction:
Time Horizon:

Thesis:
- clear and simple explanation

Why Now:
- why the opportunity exists now

Risks:
- key things that could go wrong

Invalidation:
- what would prove this thesis wrong

Confidence:
- 0 to 1 score

---

## Watch Conditions (only if WATCH)
- what confirmation is missing
- what would upgrade to RECOMMEND

---

## Why Pass (only if PASS)
- why no edge exists
- whether it is noise, priced in, or too uncertain
